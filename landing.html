ABOUT MAGNATE.

MAGNATE is a narrative and content marketing agency for industry leaders who need to stand out with integrity in a crowded market.

We specialize in developing unique stories and content strategies for B2B companies that drive results: revenue, fundraising, industry notoriety.

We work closely with your team to build a GTM story that is shared across your organization and positions you as the only smart choice in your market.

Now accepting clients for narrative & thought leadership strategy.

MAGNATE was founded by a group of business journalists, media strategists and brand developers.

Our CEO Ross spent over a decade working with founders and growth-stage companies, and along the way built three companies, and sold one. Current and former clients include Red Bull, Live Nation and City of Los Angeles.

We built MAGNATE to help good companies cut through the noise and find a strategic edge in the online wilderness.
